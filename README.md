# Hi, I'm Sven 👋

I am a Senior Information Security Architect working on secure architectures, complex systems, and regulated environments.

My professional work often begins where requirements are still incomplete, technical boundaries are unclear, and several project phases have already started talking to each other.

I am particularly interested in how architecture, discovery, engineering, and knowledge governance can help turn complex problem spaces into systems that are understandable, implementable, and operable.

## What I work on

- Information security and solution architecture
- Secure and regulated infrastructure environments
- Technical discovery and requirements analysis
- AI-assisted engineering
- Engineering knowledge and decision governance
- Self-hosted systems, automation, and open-source experiments

## Current projects

### Engineering Knowledge Core

A research and engineering project about shared orientation between humans and machines.

Its central question is how knowledge, discovery, governance, and falsifiable engineering decisions can be represented so that both humans and AI systems can work with them.

Currently developed in a private repository while the foundations are still being stabilized.

### Family OS

A self-hosted family organization and assistance platform combining calendars, reminders, voice interaction, automation, and local AI services.

Currently developed privately.

### CBOM

An experiment around identifying and documenting cryptographic assets and dependencies across systems.

Currently developed privately.

## How I work

I prefer architecture before accidental complexity, explicit decisions before hidden assumptions, and systems that remain understandable after the first successful demo.

Most projects start with a problem that looked smaller at first.
